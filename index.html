About This Site

This is an Audi fansite where you can find out about the latest Audi car models and see stunning images of their breathtaking designs. Since the car company was established in 1909 in Germany, the brand has grown exponentially and is now regarded as one of the top car brands in the World. Models such as the Audi 80 and the R8 have brought great success throughout the years.
