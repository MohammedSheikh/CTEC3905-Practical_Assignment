HISTORY OF AUDI

Audi Timeline

1909

The Audi name is born

After leaving the company named after him, August Horch creates “August Horch Automobilwerke GmbH” but is forced to rename the rival company.

1965

The 73-hp Audi with four-stroke engine developed

The 73-hp Audi with four-stroke engine developed from the DKW F 102 marks the end of the two-stroke era.

1977

The popular Audi 100 Avant debuts

The Audi 100 Avant is a new arrival on the market.

2004

Audi introduces daytime-running LED

Audi becomes the first manufacturer to feature peripheral white LEDs as daytime running lights on the Audi A8 W12

2009

The stop-start system is introduced

The start-stop system is introduced in the second quarter of the year. The system shuts down the engine once the car has come to a stop, the gear lever stands at idle and the driver releases the clutch pedal.
